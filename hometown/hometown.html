Bogor City

This is my hometown, Kota Bogor, West Java. I was born there and lived for almost 20 years now.

Bogor mostly known by people as raining city because as it named, the frequent of rain in Bogor is insane. It almost impossible for us seeing Bogor not raining for more than two wweks Beside of that fact, Bogor also one of the biggest city in terms of population and workflow. It's a part of Jaboddetabek which are the metropolitan cities in Indonesia.

For many people including me, Bogor has many memories in it. The foods, the places, and most importantly the people in it are irreplaceable. If you want to know more about it you can check my explanation on local food and tourist attraction.
