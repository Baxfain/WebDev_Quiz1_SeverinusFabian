Local Foods

Bogor has many traditional food start from appetizer, main course, to desert. Here are some of the food that you should try at least once when you visit Bogor:


  • Cungkring

    Cungkring is an appetizer made from the tongue, lips, and leg muscles of cow that combined with lontong and coated with peanut sauce. It has salty and savory flavour with various textures depending on which part of the meat we eat.

    Rp20.000 - Rp50.000
  • Asinan Bogor

    Asinan bogor is made from various vegetables and fruits that combined with vinegar and also chilli. It has the combination of sour and spicy with the fresness of the fruits in it. Usually it also accompanied by a special crackers made for Asinan Bogor Itself

    Rp25.000 - Rp50.000
  • Soto Kuning Bogor

    Soto Kuning Bogor is a meat soto made with turmeric and coconut milk as the based of its soup. It contains dice shaped meat and sometimes people add tripe and tendon on it. It has a strong salty and also savory falvour that usually combined with rice to make a perfect combination.

    Rp15.000 - Rp40.000
  • Soto Mie Bogor

    Soto Mie Bogor is a unique soto. When usually people eat soto with rice, this soto is eaten with noodles. Even thought not a few people also eat it with rice. In it, we can get meat, rissoles, tomatoes, and also radish as it main condiments. The unique savory and texture combination from all the condiments make this soto become a comfort food for Bogorian.

    Rp15.000 - Rp30.000
