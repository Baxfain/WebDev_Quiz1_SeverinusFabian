Tourist Places

Not only has great foods, Bogor also become people's favorite to visit for healing. The great air and trees here become a special attraction for people esplecially from Jabodetabek that full of industrial buildings. Maybe the most popular one is Puncak Bogor where you can really get the atmosphere of nature directly. But not only that, here I listed some other destination to visit especially in the Bogor City itsel:


  • Kebun Raya Bogor

    Kebun Raya Bogor placed literally in the middle of Bogor City where here we can see a lot of trees, labolatory, museum, and even the palace for the president. People say that it's the heart of Bogor City and also the area around it because it's actually a small forest inside a city. People usually go here to work out, run, have a picnic, or even just for a walk, enjoying the view, the air, and the vibes. Outside of the Kebun Raya itself, there are sidewalks that many people use for them that don't want to get in into Kebun Raya Bogor and still can feel the vibes for free. It's a family friendly place and smoke free so it's a nice place to visit.

    Rp0 - Rp20.000
  • Suryakencana

    It's a China Town in Bogor that become Bogor's landmark for a long time. Here, you will find a vibes that are totally different from usual place in Bogor. The main attraction here is the culinary that are available all along the road for around 5KM. There, you can find all Bogor's local food from the viral one to the hidden once for example from Soto Kuning Bogor to Bir Kotjok. Also, this is the place where pork are normal to get, not like the other place in Bogor so many Non Muslim people from Bogor and even tourists come to this place to try that. But don't worry because there are also many Halal food stall here to try.

    Rp0.000 - Rp60.000
  • Sentul

    It's actually outside from Bogor City, to be exact, it's in Bogor Regency, but many tourists come to this place and take it as a part of Bogor. The main attraction here is the modern but yet traditional environtment combined. The geography of it which are on foot of a mountain make this place has a fresh air and good views. Here you can see a lot of cafes with modern architecture that offers the views of nature. You can visit this place just for enjoying the vibes for free or try some cafes, mall, and also food stall.

    Rp0 - Rp100.000
